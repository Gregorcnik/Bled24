About

This workshop is devoted to the symmetries of graphs, and in particular, various aspects of generating and mantaining datasets of graphs of high level of symmetry. The workshop is organised by the Society of mathematicians, physicists and astronomers of Slovenia, and is a part of the research activities within the ARIS research project J1 4351. The programme will consist of several open problem and research sessions. Participation is by invitation only.
