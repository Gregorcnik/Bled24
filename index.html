About

This workshop is devoted to symmetries of graphs, and in particular, various aspects of generating and maintaining datasets of graphs with high level of symmetry. The workshop is organised by the Society of Mathematicians, Physicists and Astronomers of Slovenia (DMFA Slovenije), and is a part of research activities within the ARIS research project J1 4351. The programme will consist of several open problem sessions and other research activities. Participation is by invitation only.
